Palworld map image, coordinate transform, and map point data
Source: https://github.com/RNZ01/palworld-server-dashboard
Revision: 9db699751df9206f734feba30b928bc309e819ff

The map image in this directory is a WebP conversion of
public/palworld-map/full-map-z4.png. The fast-travel coordinates are stored in
paledit/data/map_points.json.
